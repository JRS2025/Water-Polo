Rules you need to follow

Lets go over some basic rules for water polo


1. No scratching -------------------- 9. No drowning

2. No trash-talking ---------------- 10. No cusing

3. No biting ------------------------- 11. No aruging with referee

4. No punching --------------------- 12. No aruging with coach

5. No elbowing --------------------- 13. Don't hit goalie

6. No slapping ---------------------- 14. Be a good sport

7. No touching the cage ---------- 15. Don't fight with players

8. No kicking off of other players


Here are 2 rules about the ball

No two hands on ball( only exception is goalie )

No ball under the water


here are some other rules you can know

1.If you bleed, you must leave the pool

2. One whistle: minor/ordinary foul (ofense keeps the ball)

3. Two whistles: ofensive foul (defense gets the ball)

4. Three (or more) whistles: major/personal foul (offense keeps the ball; shot clock resets; defender is ejected for 20 seconds)
