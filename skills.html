What are some skills you might need

Many skills are going to be needed whenever you are playing, you might not need all of them, but it's best to learn them all.

  1. 2 years of swimming or more

  2. 1 year of eggbeatering

  3. 1 year of ball handling

  4. train your endurence to withstand a punch

  5. train your physical strength to at least lift 100 pounds

  6. learn all of the calls

There aren't many skills that you will need in this sport but it's always best to master them while you can so you can easily take on other players and overpower them
